About me
I’m a DevOps Engineer who works on building and managing infrastructure and deployment pipelines. I’ve worked with CI/CD tools like GitHub Actions and Jenkins, and I use Terraform to manage infrastructure as code.

My focus is on building systems that are scalable, reliable, and maintainable.
